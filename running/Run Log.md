## How to Log a Run

After each run, tell Claude:
1. **Date** of the run
2. **Actual distance** (miles)
3. **Total time** (min) -- including walk breaks
4. **Average heart rate** (bpm) -- from your watch
5. **Average pace** (min/mile) -- from your watch
6. **Effort score** (1-10, where 10 = maximum effort)
7. **Run/walk ratio** used (e.g., Run 2 min / Walk 1 min) -- Phase 1 only
8. **Notes** (optional -- how you felt, weather, pain/discomfort, anything notable)

Claude will update this log and adjust the upcoming training plan based on your performance.
